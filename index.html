Vicious

Sound card for Apple-1 computer. Based on SID and CIA chips.

Features

  • SID Chip for sound generation. SID Chip is the sound chip used in Commodore 64.
  • CIA Chip for timed interrupt generation. Without timer there could be no rhythm.
  • Onboard ROM for firmware: Sid Tune player and an API.
